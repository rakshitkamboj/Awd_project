JEE Main Collage Predictor

JEE main college predictor is a collection of rank wise list of NITs, IIITs, and other Government funded institutes since 2014. All data is carefully obtained from JOSAA official website after the final round. The JEE main cutoff is the most exhaustive JEE cutoff tool, which is free, and no registration is needed.

JEE Main is conducted by NTA and admission counseling is conducted by JOSAA. Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) conducts counseling B.Tech admissions in NITs, IIITs, and other institutes based on JEE Mains. There are 7 rounds of admissions and all rounds cutoff (both opening and closing ranks) are presented rank wise, college wise, and course wise in the cutoff tool. We also have important tools Like JEE Main Rank Predictor, JEE Advance Rank Predictor and JEE advanced College Predictor. There are two JEE papers conducted. JEE Main paper 1 is for admission into engineering courses and paper 2 is for architecture and planning related subjects. JOSAA conducts undergrad admission for both the papers. Therefore, ranks related to architecture and planning are based on paper 2 and other subject cutoffs are for JEE main paper 1. This will answer which college can I get with xyz rank or percentile in JEE mains.

JEE Main College Predictor Key Features

  1. Opening and closing ranks for all Categories (OC, EWS, OBC, SC, and ST). It also includes PWD (Persons with Disability) for JEE Mains.
  2. JEE cutoff for both Gender Neutral and Female only seats.
  3. College wise, course wise JEE Main cutoff for each NIT, IIIT, GFTIs.
  4. All India (IIITs, and other institutes), Home State, and Other State cut off.
